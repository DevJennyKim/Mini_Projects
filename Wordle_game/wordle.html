How to play?

  1. Put the letter into the box. Than click the submit!
  2. Check the result
    • Green : The letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
    • Yellow : The letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
    • Gray : The letter is not in the word in any spot.
  3. Put the letter into the box again and submit!
  4. Repeat the step 1 - 3 until you get the answer.
  5. You have 6 chance to guess.
